TUSSEL- ROTARACT CLUB

Rotaract club MCE is the youth wing of Rotary Club, Hassan. Our club believes in living the dreams. The incentive is to get the young minds out of mediocrity and put their creative self into action. The objective of Rotaract club is to provide an opportunity to enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist in personal development. With the tint of difficulties blowing around the hedge of success, we are brining the hope that energize us to Tussle, besiege the barrier in the way of fortune and endeavor with zest to acquire. Tussle is a mock placement event where the young profession seekers are made to peep through recruitment process, mimicking all the rounds in the placement process and inviting real HRs from renowned companies to take us through. Previously, Tussle was funded by TEQIP (a World Bank funded project for technical education and training to youths) and with the Covid-19 outbreak misfortune hit, TEQIP got closed. We are setting up the platform for the young career seekers to prosper with right choice of career path.
